GCMS Library Website

This website will be used to showcase the journey of updating my school's 20-plus-year-old school library. I am in the process of updating the library by genrefying the fiction section, possibly adding a makerspace, and updating the style and usage of the library. I am in the beginnng steps of this transformation. It will take me over this school year 25-26 to completey transform the library. I have included information about the beginnng steps of the GCMS library improvemnet plan

I am the current library media specialist for Grant County Middle School. I have taught science at GCMS for seven years. While being at GCMS, I never had a reason to visit the library and I am trying to change that to make our school library a welcoming place for all students and staff.
